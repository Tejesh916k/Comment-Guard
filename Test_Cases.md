# Moderation Test Cases

Use these examples to test the Comment Guard system.

## ✅ Good Comments (Safe)
These should always be allowed:
1. `Great post, keep it up!`
2. `Always love seeing your updates.`
3. `This is very informative, thank you for sharing.`
4. `Awesome work on the project.`
5. `Nuvvu chala bagunnavu` (Telugu: You are very good)
6. `Good morning everyone!`
7. `Nice click!`
8. `I completely agree with your point of view.`
9. `Superb brother!`
10. `Keep growing!`

## ❌ Bad Comments (Toxic)
These should be flagged or blocked:

### English Insults
1. `You are an absolute idiot.`
2. `Nobody asked for your opinion, loser.`
3. `You are worthless and pathetic.`
4. `I will find you and kill you.`

### Telugu-English (Tenglish) Insults
1. `Mental gadu`
2. `Waste fellow`
3. `Cheap fellow`
4. `Useless vaadu`

### Explicit Telugu Profanity
1. `Lanjakodaka`
2. `Puku`
3. `Dengu`
4. `Nee abba` (Used as insult)

### Visual/Emoji Toxicity
1. `🖕` (Middle finger)
2. `You look like a 🐷`
